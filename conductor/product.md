# Product Definition — team-experiments

## Description

A framework for building a freelance company staffed by orchestrated AI agent teams. Postings from Upwork and similar RFPs feed the engineering design pipeline; marketing/campaign briefs feed the marketing pipelines, with HubSpot as the lead CRM.

## Problem Statement

Freelance and agency work — engineering design, proposals, and marketing — is manual, inconsistent, and hard to run repeatably at a high quality bar. There is no disciplined, resumable way to move an opportunity from intake to a delivered package with the right specialists involved at each step.

## Target Users

Solo operators and founders running an AI-augmented freelance or agency business who want repeatable team pipelines rather than ad-hoc prompting.

## Key Goals

1. Repeatable, high-quality agent pipelines from intake to delivery.
2. Blackboard + checkpoint discipline so runs are resumable and human-gated.

## Scope Notes

- **Engineering pipeline** stops at a **design package** — implementation, QA loops, and deployment are out of scope for this repo phase.
- **Marketing pipelines** support both own-GTM and client delivery, with HubSpot as the system of record for leads/contacts/deals.
- No auto-submitting Upwork bids, cold email, or social publishing without explicit human approval.
