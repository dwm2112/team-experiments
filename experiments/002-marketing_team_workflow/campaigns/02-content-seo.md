## Claim lexicon (allowed / banned / soften)

Use this lexicon for all public copy (LinkedIn, waitlist, SEO pages, nurture, discovery talk tracks). Charter language does **not** ship outbound until claim/legal review (`claim reviewer [TBD]`) clears it.

### Allowed (default market language)

| Prefer | Use when |
|--------|----------|
| trustworthy digital coworker | Hero / category framing |
| AI teammate / AI coworker | Short-form social |
| scoped agentic assistant | Product/offer clarity |
| custom-built for your workflows | Differentiation vs commodity chatbots |
| plans and executes **defined** workflows | Capability without unbounded autonomy |
| alongside humans / with clear guardrails | Trust + safety |
| human-in-the-loop / human decides on critical steps | Objection handling |
| First Agent Scoping / founding partner waitlist | Offer wedge |
| fit your stack, then improve | Interoperability + iteration |
| predictable, transparent, responsible | Values (from charter, claim-safe) |

### Soften (never alone; always pair with oversight)

| Charter / internal phrase | Public soften |
|---------------------------|---------------|
| autonomous AI agents | scoped agents that act within agreed boundaries |
| digital employees | digital coworkers / AI teammates |
| tireless, continuously learning | reliable on defined tasks; improves with feedback loops |
| managing operations | supporting defined ops workflows with human handoffs |
| every business | small organizations / teams like yours (SMB-primary) |

**Rule:** If a sentence implies unbounded autonomy or employment replacement, rewrite before publish.

### Banned (public kickoff)

- fully autonomous / runs the business alone  
- replaces employees / replaces your team / fire your staff  
- guaranteed ROI / guaranteed results / any business, any workflow  
- Robotics Division / Intelligence Division / multi-division synergy claims in kickoff creative  
- invented logos, customers, case studies, win rates, or performance metrics  
- “autonomous digital employees” as a headline or CTA (internal charter only until claim review)  
- surveillance / intelligence-product framing; robotics framing  

### Soften patterns (approved constructions)

1. **Capability + boundary:** “Agents that plan and execute *defined* workflows — with human approval on critical decisions.”  
2. **Augmentation hero:** “Built to work with you, not around you.”  
3. **Scope honesty:** “Custom coworker for a painful, repeatable process — not a generic chatbot.”  
4. **Anti–lock-in:** “Designed to fit the tools you already use; iterate after Week 1.”  

### Claim review gate

No public autonomy, employment, or ROI phrasing without named reviewer sign-off. Same blocker twice → escalate to human (anti-loop).

## Visual / brand direction notes (for design package — not full brand book)

Direction only for designers; not a complete identity system.

### Signal

- **Brand-first:** “Atlas Sovereign” / Agentic AI Division must read as a hero-level signal on waitlist and cornerstone — not a nav-only mark.  
- **One composition:** Landing first viewport = brand + one headline + one short support line + one CTA group + one dominant visual plane (workflow/coworker metaphor). No stat strips, fake logos, or dashboard clutter.  
- **Metaphor:** Human + scoped agent collaboration (handoff, checklist, gates) — not robots, not surveillance, not glowing “AGI” clichés.

### Look & feel (avoid AI-default clusters)

- Prefer calm, precise, operator-credible atmosphere (subtle depth/gradient or real workplace/tool context) over purple-glow SaaS or terracotta-cream editorial clichés.  
- Typography: purposeful, distinctive pairing; avoid Inter/Roboto/Arial as the brand voice.  
- Color: define a small CSS variable set (ink, paper, one accent, one trust/success). High contrast for accessibility.  
- Motion (if any): 2–3 intentional cues (e.g., propose→decide→execute sequence, gate unlock) — presence, not noise.

### Diagram standards

- Propose → Decide → Execute: three clear steps; human node visually owns “Decide.”  
- 3 gates: numbered, scannable, usable as LinkedIn carousel frames.  
- No floating promo badges on hero imagery.

### Division lock

Wordmarks, footers, and OG images: **Agentic AI Division** only. No Robotics/Intelligence marks or “synergy” visuals in kickoff.
