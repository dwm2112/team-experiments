# Campaign Brief: Atlas Sovereign — Agentic AI Kickoff Brand + Campaign

**Experiment:** `experiments/002-marketing_team_workflow/`  
**Pipeline:** marketing-client  
**Date:** 2026-07-24T01:00:00Z  
**Client / brand:** Atlas Sovereign — Agentic AI Division

## Objective

Establish a credible **Agentic AI Division** brand beachhead and a focused kickoff motion that converts early interest into a **qualified waitlist + discovery-call pipeline**—not broad awareness and not multi-division corporate branding.

Success for this engagement = board-approved **branding + campaign design package** ready for founder-led execution (no site implementation).

## North Star Metric

**Qualified waitlist signups who book a discovery call** (or accept a calendar invite) within 14 days of signup.

**ASSUMPTION — working targets (kickoff window):** 40–60 waitlist signups → 12–18 discovery conversations → 4–6 sales-qualified opportunities. Secondary proxies: LinkedIn follows from ICP titles; engagement on founder posts. Do **not** optimize for impressions alone.

## Audience / ICP

**Primary (kickoff):** SMB operators (≈10–100 employees; owner / COO / ops lead) with painful, repeatable ops work who want **custom-built agentic assistants** inside existing tools—not generic chatbot SaaS.

- Trigger: hiring lag, tool sprawl, “need another person but can’t hire yet”
- Disqualifier: plug-and-play commodity AI only; expects “replace my team”

**Secondary order:** (1) Nonprofit program/ops leads → (2) Freelance / solo (later wave; lower ACV for custom builds)

## Positioning & Offer

**Positioning:** Atlas Sovereign’s Agentic AI Division builds **custom, trustworthy AI coworkers** for small organizations—agents that plan and execute defined workflows alongside humans, with clear guardrails. Not robotics, not intelligence/surveillance, not “AI that replaces judgment.”

**Hero (kickoff):** Custom AI coworkers for small teams—built to work with you, not around you.

**Offer wedge:** **Founding partner waitlist** → short application → discovery call → scoped pilot / “First Agent Scoping” conversation.

**Claim-safe rules:** Prefer *digital coworker / AI teammate / scoped agentic assistant*. Avoid *fully autonomous, replaces employees, guaranteed ROI, any business*. Soften charter “autonomous digital employees” in market copy; always pair capability with human oversight on critical decisions.

**Messaging house (summary):**
1. Hero — coworker framing + scoped workflows + human handoffs  
2. Proof — workflow maps, principles, process story (no invented logos/metrics)  
3. Objections — replacement fear, overpromise, too-small/not-technical, lock-in, autonomy myths

## Content Pillars

1. **Trustworthy digital coworkers** — predictability, transparency, scoped autonomy. Assets: “what digital employee means (and doesn’t)”; principles one-pager; “3 gates before an agent acts.”
2. **Human-in-the-loop by design** — augmentation hero; replacement language banned. Assets: Agent proposes → human decides → agent executes diagram; FAQ objection cluster.
3. **Built to fit your stack—then improve** — interoperability + iteration. Assets: First Agent Scoping checklist; Week 1 / Month 1 loop; anti–lock-in note.
